how to make pizza

You can serve your pizzas made-to-order,

or go with the easier route: Build a buffet of pizza making supplies.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps to take

  1. Pizza Stone and Preheat Grill:

    Place your pizza stone or steel (or a sheet of aluminum foil) directly on the grill grates. Close cover and preheat the grill on high heat.

  2. Slide Pizza Onto Stone:

    Once the grill is heated, use a pizza peel that is dusted with flour or cornmeal to slide

    assembled pizza onto your stone or foil. Close the grill cover.

  3. Grill:

    Grill the pizza until the crust is browned,

    about 10 to 12 minutes. Use either a pair of tongs or a pizza peel to remove the pizza from the grill, and slice
